About Me

Prior to beginning my journey in software development, I was working in Education. I enjoyed the opportunities that teaching gave me to build my leadership and people skills. Teaching allowed me to solve ever-evolving complex problems while working in a team environment. During my time earning a license in Middle School Math, I fell in love with the way that software development combines logical and analytical skills with creativity. This love has continued to grow as I employ my favorite skills that I learned working in education with every new technology I encounter.
